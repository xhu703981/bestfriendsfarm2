# Best Friends Farm

**Bring warmth, companionship, and a touch of AI magic to your Terraria world!**

## 🐾 Mod Features & Mechanisms

- **Adoptable Animals:**  
  Friendly dogs, cats, and cows will spawn in your world, each waiting to become your loyal companion.

- **Bonding System:**  
  Build a special relationship with each animal by feeding, chatting, and spending time together. Your bond level increases as you care for them, unlocking new tiers of friendship and unique buffs!

- **Feeding Mechanics:**  
  - **Dog:** Loves bones! Feed your dog bones to grow closer.
  - **Cat:** A true fish connoisseur—any kind of fish will win your cat’s heart.
  - **Cow:** Nothing beats fresh hay for your gentle cow.
  - Each animal only accepts its favorite food, so stock up and show your love!

- **Conversation Powered by LLM (AI):**  
  Thanks to integrated Large Language Model (LLM) technology, you can actually talk to your animals!  
  - Open the animal interaction UI, type a message, and your pet will reply in its own unique, adorable style.
  - Dogs are playful and silly, cats are sassy and aloof, and cows are gentle and always mention grass or “moo”!
  - Every conversation is powered by real AI, making each interaction feel alive and personal.

- **Animal Interaction UI:**  
  A custom, easy-to-use interface lets you feed, chat, and bond with your animals.  
  - Right-click your animal to open the UI.
  - Choose to feed or talk, and watch your friendship grow!

- **Buffs & Bonding Tiers:**  
  As your bond deepens, your animal will grant you special buffs:
  - **Dog:** +5 max life and +10 life regen when bonded.
  - **Cat:** +10% movement speed when bonded.
  - **Cow:** +6 defense when bonded.
  - Higher bond levels unlock stronger effects and show your animal’s true affection!

- **Bonding Progression:**  
  - Feed your animal daily to maintain and increase your bond.
  - Streaks and favorite foods give bonus bonding points.
  - Neglecting your animal will slowly decrease your bond—don’t forget to visit your friends!

## 🌟 Why Best Friends Farm?

Every hero deserves a companion. Every adventure is brighter with a loyal friend by your side.  
With Best Friends Farm, your Terraria journey will never be lonely—let the cuddles, purrs, and moos fill your world with joy!

# 好朋友农场

**为你的泰拉瑞亚世界带来温暖、陪伴，还有一丝AI的魔法！**

## 🐾 主要特色与机制

- **可收养的动物：**  
  可爱的狗、猫和奶牛会在你的世界中出现，成为你忠实的伙伴。

- **羁绊系统：**  
  通过喂食、聊天和陪伴，与每只动物建立特殊的关系。随着你悉心照料，羁绊等级会提升，解锁新的友谊阶段和独特的增益！

- **喂食机制：**  
  - **狗：** 最爱骨头！用骨头喂狗可以增进感情。
  - **猫：** 真正的鱼类美食家——任何鱼都能俘获猫咪的心。
  - **奶牛：** 没有什么比新鲜的干草更让奶牛开心。
  - 每只动物只接受自己最喜欢的食物，记得多多准备!

- **AI对话（大语言模型）：**  
  得益于大语言模型（LLM）技术，你真的可以和动物对话！  
  - 打开动物互动界面，输入你的消息，你的宠物会用独特又可爱的风格回复你。
  - 狗狗活泼呆萌，猫咪傲娇俏皮，奶牛温柔沉稳！
  - 每一次对话都由真实AI驱动，让互动更加生动有趣。

- **动物互动界面：**  
  简洁易用的自定义界面，让你轻松喂食、聊天、增进感情。  
  - 右键点击动物即可打开界面。
  - 选择喂食或聊天，见证亲密成长！

- **增益与羁绊阶段：**  
  羁绊加深后，动物会为你带来特殊增益：
  - **狗：** 羁绊后+5最大生命值和+10生命再生。
  - **猫：** 羁绊后+10%移动速度。
  - **奶牛：** 羁绊后+6防御。
  - 更高的羁绊等级会解锁更强的效果，展现动物的真挚情感！

- **羁绊成长：**  
  - 每天喂食动物，维持并提升羁绊。
  - 连续喂食和最爱食物会获得额外羁绊点。
  - 忽视动物会导致羁绊缓慢下降——别忘了常去看看你的朋友！

## 🧠 How to Enable AI Conversation (LLM Setup)

To use the animal conversation feature, you need to run a local LLM (AI) server:

1. Download the required files from the mod’s llama_cpp folder:
   - `llama-server.exe`
   - A model file (e.g., `phi-2.Q3_K_M.gguf`)

2. Start the LLM server:
   - Double-click `llama-server.exe` (or run it from the command line).
   - Wait for the server to say it’s ready (usually shows a message like “Listening on port 8080”).

3. Make sure the mod is configured to connect to the server:
   - By default, the mod tries to connect to `http://localhost:8080`.
   - If you need to change this, edit the mod’s config file or use the in-game settings (if available).

4. Now, when you talk to your animals, you’ll get real AI-powered replies!

**Troubleshooting:**
- If you get no response, make sure the server is running and the model file is present.
- Some models require a powerful CPU or lots of RAM.
- For more help, check the mod’s Workshop page or discussion section.
