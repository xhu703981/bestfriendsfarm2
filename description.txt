Welcome to Best Friends Farm!

Adopt adorable animals—dogs, cats, and cows—who will wander your world, waiting to become your best friends. Spend time with your furry (and moo-ey) companions: feed them their favorite treats, talk to them, and build a special bond! The more you care for your animals, the more they’ll love you back, rewarding you with unique buffs and heartwarming moments.

Each animal has its own personality and favorite food. Pet, feed, and chat with them through a lovely UI, and watch your friendship grow from friendly to best friends forever! Can you become the ultimate animal buddy?

Let the cuddles, purrs, and moos begin!

Why BestFriendsFarm: Every warrior deserves companions; every journey should not be lonely; All great adventures should be accompanied by the simplest of lovelinesses.

欢迎来到好朋友农场！

可爱的动物——狗、猫和奶牛——它们会在你的世界中徘徊，等待成为你最亲密的伙伴。陪伴你毛茸茸（和“哞哞”）的朋友们：喂它们最喜欢的食物，与它们交谈，建立特别的羁绊！你越用心照顾它们，它们就会越爱你，并以独特的增益和温馨的时刻回报你。

每只动物都有自己的性格和最爱的食物。通过可爱的界面抚摸、喂食、聊天，看着你们的友谊从普通朋友成长为最好的朋友！你能成为终极动物伙伴吗？

让拥抱、呼噜和“哞哞”声响彻整个旅程吧！

为什么选择最好的朋友农场：每一位勇士都值得拥有伙伴；每一段旅程都不应孤单；所有伟大的冒险都应有最简单的美好相伴。
